### XML

```
<Person>
  <FirstName> Tim </FirstName>
  <LastName> Rayburn </LastName>
  <AddressLine1> 5445 Legacy Drive </AddressLine1>
  <AddressLine2> Suite 100 </AddressLine2>
  <City> Plano </City>
  <State> TX </State>
  <Zip> 75024 </Zip>  
</Person>
```
